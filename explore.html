Offered in conjunction with the Restaurant Association of Metropolitan Washington.Each spring across the city local restaurants celebrate spring’s arrival by showcasing their best spring menu items. This year, as our city reacts to COVID-19, The National Cherry Blossom Festival recognizes that our restaurant and bar partners are facing additional challenges. Here are some simple ways you can help!

Check out the many new grab and go and carry out options local restaurants have established. Continue to order delivery through your preferred delivery service. Purchase a gift card to your favorite restaurant for future use. Consider picking up some items from your local grocer, restaurant, or bakery.

Thanks to our 2021 Cherry Picks Partners. We had an unprecedented number of participants this year in our program. We thank them for their support and look forward to celebrating with them in the future.

Petal Porches

Washington DC Metro area residents are invited to join us in celebrating spring by participating in our Petal Porches program!
Participants are asked to fully decorate their Petal Porches from March 20 through April 17. Information on how to register your home is still to come. For inspiration, check out our online gallery from the 2021 Petal Porch Parade. In lieu of an official procession in 2022, festival-goers will be encouraged to explore DC by admiring Petal Porches across the area based on registered locations. We look forward to blooming with you!
